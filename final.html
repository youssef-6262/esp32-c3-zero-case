Final Design

The completed and improved protective case for the ESP32-C3 Zero

Final Design Overview

The final version of the case includes all the improvements made after testing Prototype 1 and Prototype 2. This design has rounded corners, a secure L-shaped sliding lock, wireless-signal shaped vents, and the correct case height to protect the pins. The lid was also adjusted to make the buttons easier to press.

Final Design Images

Final Improvements Made

  • The height of the box was increased to fully protect the bottom pins.
  • The lid thickness was reduced in the button area so the buttons are easier to press.
  • The L-shaped locking system was kept because it worked well and held the lid securely.
  • The wireless-signal shaped vents were kept to provide airflow and cooling.
  • The printer bed was cleaned and dried properly so the final print could stick and print successfully.

Conclusion

The final design is suitable because it fits the ESP32-C3 Zero correctly, protects the pins and components, and allows easy access to the buttons and charging port. It also includes a secure L-shaped locking system and ventilation slots for airflow and cooling. After improving the height of the case and reducing the lid thickness, the final version became stronger, easier to use, and more practical for classroom use.
