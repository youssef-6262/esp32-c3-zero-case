Prototype 2

The second version with improved dimensions and an L-shaped locking system

Prototype 2 Overview

In Prototype 2, I applied the feedback from the first test. I increased the internal dimensions so the ESP32-C3 Zero fits perfectly. I also widened the USB and pin openings. For security, I introduced an L-shaped sliding lock on the lid. While the fit was much better, new issues with the case height and lid thickness were discovered during testing.

Design Images

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • The board fits perfectly inside the case.
  • The USB and pin openings are large enough.
  • The new L-shaped locks hold the lid securely.
  • The wireless-signal shaped vents look great.

Cons

  • The case height is too low, so the bottom pins stick out and could bend.
  • The lid is too thick, making the top buttons very hard to press.

Planned Improvements

For the final design, I need to increase the height of the walls to fully cover and protect the bottom pins. I also need to reduce the thickness of the lid over the button area so the user can press them easily.
