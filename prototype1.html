Prototype 1

The first printed version of the ESP32-C3 Zero protective case

Prototype 1 Overview

Prototype 1 was my first printed test version. I used it to check the overall shape, the size of the case, and how well the lid matched the base. This version showed that the case shape was correct, but the internal space and openings still needed improvement before the board could fit properly.

Design Images

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • The overall shape of the case was correct.
  • The print quality was good.
  • The walls felt strong and solid.

Cons

  • The internal size was too small for the board.
  • The board could not fit inside the case.
  • The USB opening was too tight.
  • The bottom slots were too narrow for the connectors.
  • The lid was printed without locks in this early test.

Planned Improvements

After testing Prototype 1, I planned to increase the internal dimensions, enlarge the USB opening, widen the bottom pin slots, and improve the lid design in the next version.
